# Phishing Attack Remediation

This playbook outlines the procedures for investigating phishing attacks targeting enterprise users.

---

## Detection

Phishing incidents may be detected through:

User reports  
Suspicious login alerts  
Email security alerts  

---

## Investigation Procedure

### Step 1 – Review Email Message

1. Open Microsoft 365 Defender
2. Navigate to Threat Explorer
3. Locate the suspicious email

---

### Step 2 – Review Authentication Activity

Check sign-in logs for suspicious login attempts.

---

### Step 3 – Review Mailbox Rules

Verify no malicious forwarding rules were created.

---

## Containment Actions

Reset user credentials  
Remove malicious mailbox rules  
Block malicious sender domains  

---

## Post Incident Actions

User phishing awareness training  
Review email filtering rules
